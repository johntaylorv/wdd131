CyberByte Site Plan

Author: John Taylor Vaillincourt

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to help everyday users understand and apply basic cybersecurity practices to stay safe online. It aims to break down complex topics like passwords, scams, and privacy into simple, actionable tips.

Audience

The audience includes students, parents, and working professionals with little to no technical background who want to improve their digital safety and become more confident using technology securely.

Dynamic Elements

  • A random "Cyber Tip of the Day" displayed from a list of tips
  • Expandable FAQ section (click to show/hide answers)
  • Filterable list of tools/resources (e.g., by platform or purpose)

Logo

A clean, modern shield or lock icon will be used as the site logo to represent protection and digital security. Designed using Canva.

Colors

  • Primary: #1E1E2F (Dark Navy)
  • Accent: #4CBB17 (Lime Green)
  • Background: #F5F5F5 (Light Gray)
  • Text: #222222 (Dark Gray/Black)

Fonts

  • Heading Font: Orbitron (tech-style)
  • Body Font: Open Sans (clean and readable)

Content

Home Page: Overview of the site mission, featured tip, and quick links to tools and guides.

Beginner Page: A beginner's guide to recognizing phishing emails, with screenshots, tips, and an interactive quiz.
